## Overview

Serving as a mentor at **NPower**, a nonprofit that creates pathways to economic prosperity by launching underserved young adults and military veterans into meaningful IT careers.

## Responsibilities

- **Advisor and Guide:** Provided advice and guidance, helping mentee(s) navigate challenges and set realistic goals based on my experience in data engineering, medical claims, and software quality assurance
- **Role Model:** Demonstrated professional behavior and ethical standards, inspiring mentees by exemplifying the qualities necessary for success in various fields
- **Supporter and Encourager:** Offered emotional support and encouragement, helping mentees build confidence and resilience during difficult times and setbacks
- **Resource Provider:** Connected mentees with valuable resources, networks, and opportunities, helping them expand their knowledge and professional network

## Topics Covered

- IT troubleshooting and proactive system support
- Career readiness and professional development
- Data engineering fundamentals
- Software quality assurance best practices
- Resume building and interview preparation

Developed a full-stack AI-powered health platform with LLM-based symptom analysis using Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash for intelligent health assessments and recommendations.

Implemented generative AI flows for symptom normalization and health history summarization, with comprehensive user authentication and Stripe subscription management.

**Tech Stack:** Next.js 15, TypeScript, Google Genkit AI, Gemini 2.0 Flash LLM, Firebase, Stripe, Radix UI, Tailwind CSS, React Query, Firestore.
